Access

  • Genre: Match three
  • Engine: Game Maker Studio 1.4
  • Language: GML
  • Team size: 4
  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Role: Programmer and Sound designer

Access is a match three game made in game maker 1.4 and is developed by me and 3 other students.

It’s a fast pace, nerve wracking game, where the objective is to survive as long as you can against an terrifying alien.

Contributed with: most of the time was spent creating the match three system, this was a combined effort from most of the team. Besides the match three system i created the sounds and implemented music and sound.
