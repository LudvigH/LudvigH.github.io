Cape VS Aliens

  • Genre: 2D Topdown Arenashooter
  • Engine: Game Maker Studio 1.4
  • Language: GML
  • Team size: 5
  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Role: Programmer and Sound Designer

Cape Vs Alien is an 2D Topdown Arenashooter where your objective is to survive waves of robot hordes by skillfully dodging and sharp shooting your way through the level. We figured enemies and weapon mechanics would take up much of the time so in the hopes of saving some time we choose to just make one map in an arena style, with doors that would spaw in different places for some varition.

In the end we made a cool litle game with some solid shooting and movement, a good and fun experience.

Contributed with: weapon system, randomised arena, system logic and graphics.
